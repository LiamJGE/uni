PROJECTS

Paddle Experience

Paddle Experience was a project for ISW, a subject in my second year of the degree in the UPV. The objective of the project was to build a persistent Paddle booking management system where a user can manage their bookings, choosing which court and time they want to play depending on availability. Each user could log into their personal account and make or delete bookings. For this project we were requested to use Java, JavaFX and CSS.

FastUKTV

FastUKTV is a Windows application designed to aid users outside of the UK who struggle with modern day applications to access their favourite TV channels and streaming services. The way FastUKTV works is by connecting to a VPN server (that I had set up) based in the UK on start-up. Then users can navigate through the minimalist and basic menus to access their desired channel or streaming services. When a user clicks on one of the options, they are redirected to the exact URL of that option or, in the case of Sky Go, it opens the Sky Go program for Windows. This project was programmed with XAML and C#.

Things To Do In Quarantine

Things To Do In Quarantine is a website that I built during the first lockdown in Spain. I started this project because I needed something to do during the first few of quarantine and I thought that other people would be also looking for new hobbies or activities to do at home.
